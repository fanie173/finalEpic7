About

Epik 7 Communications was envisaged in 2005 when four innovative black entrepreneurs subscribed to a vision of a truly black empowered marketing and communications agency.

  • 2005

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Today, the dreams of Obrey Khupe (marketing director), Sivuyile Gulwa (operations director), Nolu- siso Kelembe (financial director) and Leigh Joseph (projects director) have been realised through the launch of Epik 7 Media Communications cc

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    The four directors bring with them extensive experience and expertise across the areas of marketing, communications, print & digital media; and publishing. The company has in place a hi-tech infrastruc- ture which enables it to deliver on its pay-off line: You dream it - we'll do it.

  • December 2012

    Transition to Full Service

    At Epik 7, we strive to build collaborative relationships with our clients that enable us to grow their brand and communications profile to achieve maximum exposure. To do this, we listen carefully to our clients so we can translate their needs into a comprehensive strategy that includes integrated and consistent messaging across multiple mediums.

  • July 2014

    Expectations

    For us, a job well done is one where we've exceeded our clients' expectations and helped build their brand to a point where they stand out clearly in the market place. What we offer is in-depth expertise and integrated delivery - it will save you time and it will bring you success.
